About TradeSkills

Our Mission

TradeSkills was created with a simple mission: to enable people to exchange goods and services directly, without the need for cash. We believe in the power of community and the value of skills and resources that everyone possesses.

In a world where economic inequality continues to grow, we aim to provide an alternative system where people can meet their needs through direct exchange, building community connections in the process.

Our Story

TradeSkills began in 2024 when our founder, Andrew Green, noticed how many people in his community had valuable skills and items that others needed, but lacked the cash to make traditional purchases.

Inspired by ancient bartering systems and modern sharing economies, Andrew developed TradeSkills as a platform to facilitate these exchanges in a user-friendly, secure environment.

What started as a small local project has grown into a community of thousands of users exchanging everything from homemade goods to professional services.

Our Values

  • Community: We believe in the power of local connections and mutual support.
  • Sustainability: By encouraging reuse and sharing of resources, we reduce waste and environmental impact.
  • Accessibility: We strive to make our platform accessible to everyone, regardless of economic status.
  • Trust: We build systems that foster trust between users and with our platform.
  • Empowerment: We help people recognize and leverage the value of their skills and possessions.

How We're Different

Unlike traditional marketplaces, TradeSkills doesn't use money as the medium of exchange. Instead, we facilitate direct trades between users, allowing them to exchange goods and services based on mutual agreement of value.

We don't charge fees or take a percentage of trades. Our platform is supported by optional donations and community contributions.
