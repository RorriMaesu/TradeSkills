How TradeSkills Works

TradeSkills is a platform that allows you to exchange goods and services without using cash. Here's how to get started:

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up for a free account and complete your profile. Add information about your interests, skills, and what you're looking to trade.

2

List Your Items or Services

Create listings for items you want to trade or services you can offer. Add photos, descriptions, and specify what you're looking for in return.

3

Browse the Marketplace

Explore listings from other users to find items or services you need. Use filters to narrow down your search by category or location.

4

Make Trade Offers

When you find something you want, make a trade offer. Propose what you're willing to exchange and negotiate with the other user.

5

Complete the Trade

Once both parties agree, arrange the exchange details. Meet in person or arrange shipping depending on the items being traded.

6

Leave Feedback

After completing a trade, leave feedback for the other user. This helps build trust in the community and improves your reputation.

Trading Guidelines

  • Be Honest: Provide accurate descriptions and photos of your items.
  • Communicate Clearly: Respond promptly and be clear about your expectations.
  • Be Fair: Offer reasonable trades that provide value to both parties.
  • Stay Safe: Meet in public places for in-person exchanges or use secure shipping methods.
  • Respect Others: Be courteous and professional in all interactions.
